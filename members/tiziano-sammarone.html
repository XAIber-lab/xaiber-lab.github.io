PhD Candidate — Human Sciences for Innovation, Inclusion & Sustainability (41st Cycle), Università degli Studi Link

Integrating AI into the cyber risk management lifecycle, with human oversight kept central throughout.

Based in Rome, Tiziano works at the intersection of artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and risk management. His PhD research develops a framework for applying AI across the full risk management lifecycle — from identification and assessment to mitigation and continuous monitoring — while keeping human oversight and ethical standards central to the process.
